What can I do here?

On this website, you have the opportunity to plan your own funeral. You can choose your burial method, write letters for your loved ones, print them out, and much more. If you need help, please click on the Grim Reaper and the help page will appear. Please feel free to contact us if you need more help or if you have questions. If you want a break to think about the stuff, you can pause by clicking the log out button to continue later. In the next step, you will create an account were you can enter all your information.
Please start by clicking the arrow.
