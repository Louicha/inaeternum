Start planning your funeral

Go on planning your funeral

When we die, our family and friends are in deep mourning. Added to this, they must also organize our funeral. But what if we don’t want a funeral where everyone wears black and listen to sad music? What if we want the people to wear colorful clothes and listen to “Highway to hell”? For this reasons we – Nils, Marco and Louisa – decide to make a Website, where you are able to plan your own funeral for our lecture “Software Engineering”. The name of the Website – like our projectblog – will be “in aeternum” which is latin and means “until the end of time”.
